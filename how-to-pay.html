How to Pay with Crypto

  1. Buy USDT (ERC20) from Binance, Coinbase, or a wallet app.
  2. Click the "Pay with Crypto" button on the product you want.
  3. Send the exact amount to the wallet address shown.
  4. Wait for payment confirmation (usually under 5 minutes).

⚠️ Only send USDT using the ERC20 network. Do not use TRC20 or BEP20.
